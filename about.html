the history

cuarto; spanish for room.

Cuarto only had one mission and that was to provide the masses with good quality furniture made from eco-friendly material such as natural wood, bamboo and recycled material. Founding members Anna-Marie Jose, Sebastian Ignacio, Lorenzo Mendoza, and Eunice Buenevista began their venture back in 1998. With a passion for design and craft, the four friends left their boring day jobs in a city called Toronto to pursue a shared dream. Cuarto began as four and has grown to something much bigger today. The first cuarto warehouse, coincidentally based in Toronto, can be found in the Distillery District. Celebrating 20+ years in the industry, our team has opened its doors to welcome artists who share the same principles and values. Our business has come to nurture crafts such as pottery, textile, sculpture and botany. We wish that our furniture enhances the homey feeling in any and every dwelling.
